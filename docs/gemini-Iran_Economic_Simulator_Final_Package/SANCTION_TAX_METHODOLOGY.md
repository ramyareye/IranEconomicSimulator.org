# Methodology: Calculating the 'Sanction Tax'
## 1. The China Oil Discount
- **Formula:** `Daily Export Volume * (Brent Price - Realized Iranian Price) * 365`
- **Source:** Track Tanker data vs. OPEC official prices.
- **Estimated Impact:** $5B - $10B annually.

## 2. Middleman Costs (Banking & Logistics)
- **Formula:** `Total Trade Volume (Export + Import) * 0.15` (Assuming 15% average cost for money laundering/hawala).
- **Impact:** Billions of dollars diverted from the national treasury to private shadow networks.

## 3. Tech & Infrastructure Stagnation
- Cost of not being able to buy GE turbines or Siemens tech, leading to power outages.

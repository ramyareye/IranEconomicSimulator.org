# Strategic Pitch for Policy Makers
## The Core Message
The Iranian regime is an **Economic Siphon**. Every dollar granted to this system is a dollar taken from regional stability and global market efficiency.

## Key Talking Points
- **Systemic Corruption:** Not an accident, but a feature to fund loyalty.
- **The "Normal Iran" Dividend:** A free Iran could lower global energy prices by X% and stop the migration wave to Europe.
- **Transparency as a Weapon:** This tool democratizes economic data, making it impossible for the regime to hide the cost of its survival.

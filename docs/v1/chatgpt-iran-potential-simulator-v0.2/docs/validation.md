# Validation / Sanity checks (draft)

Goal: ensure scenarios are plausible by comparing against peer-country ranges and historical growth episodes.

Checklist:
- Does the implied 10y GDP per capita fall within peer bands?
- Are tax-to-GDP and revenue shares plausible for the assumed governance level?
- Do oil revenue assumptions respect volume constraints?
- Sensitivity: are the top drivers reasonable (not a hidden bug)?

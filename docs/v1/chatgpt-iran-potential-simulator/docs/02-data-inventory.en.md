# Data inventory (EN) — phased

## Level 1 (must-have)
- Baseline GDP (nominal USD, year)
- Population + growth
- Investment rate (% GDP)
- Tax-to-GDP
- Government oil/gas take
- Oil exports volume + price assumption
- Non-oil exports/imports
- Baseline FDI

## Level 2 (high impact)
- Leakage proxies, corruption indices
- Competition/monopoly proxies
- Country risk premium
- Sanctions intensity proxy

## Level 3
- Labor participation, unemployment
- Skilled emigration, diaspora
- Return scenarios

## Level 4
- Religious/ideological budget lines (if available)
- Reallocation multipliers (with ranges)

## Level 5
- Peer-country benchmarks for validation

# IOC Whitepaper: The Strategic Case for a Normal Iran
## Overview
The "Iran Opportunity Cost" (IOC) project is a non-partisan, data-centric initiative to quantify the economic impact of the Islamic Republic's policies on the Iranian people and the global economy.

## Strategic Impact
1. **Global Energy Security:** Replacing ideology with trade would turn Iran into a stable energy hub for Europe and Asia.
2. **Economic Migration:** By quantifying the "Brain Drain," we show the $50B+ annual loss in human capital.
3. **Regional Stability:** Tracking the diversion of national wealth to non-state actors.

## The "Normal Iran" Thesis
A democratic Iran, integrated into the global financial system (FATF/SWIFT), is projected to achieve a sustained 7-10% GDP growth, reclaiming its position as a Top 20 global economy within a decade.
